LA Fitness

View All Gyms

LA Fitness is a popular US chain, and they've moved into Canada. LA gyms are large gyms and are moderatly priced. LA Fitness has a variety of amenities, including state of the art strength training and cardiovascular equipment, group fitness classes, basketball courts, indoor pools, and personal training. The gym accepts people of all fitness levels, both beginners and professionals, and offers a comfortable setting to accommodate different exercise requirements. LA Fitness, being open late into the night and focusing significantly on convenience, enables members to integrate their fitness goals in a warm and nurturing setting.
